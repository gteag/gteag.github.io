Who are we?

We are a group of Enterprise Architects from a variety of organizations in the Toledo area. We are dedicated to furthering Enterprise Architecture discipline and practices in Northwest Ohio. We meet on a regular basis to network and discuss topics that impact system design and integration in the enterprise.

Stuff we talk about

Generally we get together and share a meal over lively discussion about EA topics. Past topics have included the following, by way of example:

  • Integration

  • System Design

  • Capability Mapping

  • Cloud Architecture

One more thing

You don't have to be an Enterprise Architect to come out and meet up with us. If you want to find out about more about what Enterprise Architecture is, how we think, and what we do, please join us and chat!
